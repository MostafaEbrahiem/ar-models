Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Welcome to 3Dmosta. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights.

1. The Data We Collect About You

We may collect, use, store and transfer different kinds of personal data about you which we have grouped together as follows:

  • Identity Data: First name, last name, and Google profile information provided via Google Sign-In.
  • Contact Data: Email address.
  • User Content: The 3D model files (.glb) you upload to our servers for hosting.
  • Transaction Data: Details about payments to and from you, managed securely by PayPal. (We do not store your credit card details).

2. How We Use Your Data

We will only use your personal data when the law allows us to. Most commonly, we will use your personal data in the following circumstances:

  • To register you as a new customer and provide you with a Creator Dashboard.
  • To host your 3D models and generate functioning AR QR codes.
  • To process your subscription payments.

3. Data Security & Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Cloud and Firebase infrastructure. We have put in place appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way. You have full control to permanently delete your 3D models from our servers at any time via your dashboard.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party gateways (PayPal) to process payments. We do not store your financial information. We use Google Analytics to monitor website traffic.
